Privacy Policy — TCMM (Trening Centar MM)

Last updated: 2026-07-22

This application ("TCMM", "the app") is used to manage training sessions at the Trening Centar MM gym. This document explains what data the app processes.

Who we are

App owner: Trening Centar MM
Contact: vladanandjic2909@gmail.com

What data we collect

  • Administrator (gym staff): username and password used to log in to the admin section.
  • Members: first name, last name, and display name, plus their completed training history. This data is entered by gym staff; members do not register or log in themselves.
  • Exercise videos: uploaded by the administrator; used to demonstrate exercises.
  • Crash and diagnostic data: if the app encounters an error, technical information (device type, operating system version, error message) is automatically sent to Sentry to help us fix the issue. This data does not include members' personal information.

The app does not collect location data or contacts, and does not use ads or tracking analytics.

How we use the data

Data is used solely to operate the app: administrator login, searching for a member at the kiosk, and displaying their daily training plan.

Where data is stored

  • Member and training data: database (Neon / PostgreSQL).
  • Exercise videos: Cloudflare R2 (object storage).
  • Application server: Hetzner (Germany).

Communication between the app and the server is encrypted (HTTPS).

Device permissions

  • Photo/video library access: used only by the administrator when adding an exercise video. The app does not access the photo library without this action.

Data sharing

We do not sell or share data with third parties, other than the infrastructure providers listed above (Neon, Cloudflare, Hetzner, Sentry) necessary to operate the app.

Data deletion

To request deletion of a member's data or an administrator account, contact us at vladanandjic2909@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; the last update date is shown at the top.
